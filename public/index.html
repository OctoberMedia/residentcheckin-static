How Safety Check-In Works

Simple, automated wellness monitoring in 6 easy steps

1

Set Daily Check-in Time

Configure a daily check-in time for your residents (e.g., 9:00 AM) through the dashboard

2

Residents Check In

Residents call or text your facility's number before the specified check-in time to confirm they're okay

3

Automated Calls Begin

If residents don't check in on time, our system automatically calls them on any phone type

4

Press "1" to Confirm

When residents answer, they simply press "1" to check in for the day - no complex interactions

5

Security Team Alerts

After one hour of no response, we automatically alert your security team for immediate action

6

Live Dashboard Monitoring

Monitor the entire process in real-time with our comprehensive dashboard showing all resident statuses
