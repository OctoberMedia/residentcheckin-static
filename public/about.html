Our Story

IamFine was founded in 2012 by brothers Colin and Paul Hammond to help them stay connected with their mother Isobel, who lives alone in Florida. What started as a simple way to check on an elderly parent daily has grown into a service that helps tens of thousands of users across the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom.

The inspiration came from a real need - their mother Isobel, who at 99 years old continues to live independently. The brothers wanted a respectful, non-intrusive way to ensure she was okay each day without constant phone calls or visits that might feel like an invasion of her independence.

ResidentCheckin.co represents the evolution of this technology for assisted living facilities. We recognized that senior living communities face similar challenges - how to efficiently monitor resident wellness while respecting their dignity and independence. By adapting our proven platform for facility use, we're helping staff save hours each day while providing better care.

Our Mission
