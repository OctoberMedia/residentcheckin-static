Getting Started

Most facilities are up and running within 24-48 hours. Our onboarding process includes:

  • Phone number configuration and testing
  • Resident enrollment (can be done in batches)
  • Staff training on the dashboard
  • Security team notification setup
  • Test calls to ensure everything works

To get started, we need:

  • Facility contact information and preferred check-in time
  • Resident list with names and phone numbers
  • Security team contact information for alerts

No long-term contracts required. We have a 90 day cancellation clause. You can cancel at any time with 90 days notice.

How It Works

Our system works with ANY phone type - landlines, basic cell phones, flip phones, smartphones. No smartphone or internet required. Residents just need to be able to answer calls and press "1" on their keypad.

We call at the check-in time, then retry every 15 minutes for an hour. If the resident still hasn't responded after an hour, the security team is automatically notified to check on them.

Yes! Residents can check in early by:

  • Calling our toll-free number
  • Sending a text message
  • Using our custom smartphone Big Button App
  • Letting the security team know they're okay (staff can check them in manually)

Staff can manually check in residents directly from the dashboard when they see them in person. This immediately stops any automated calls and updates the system.

Our vacation management system makes it easy:

  • Schedule vacations, family visits, or medical stays in advance
  • Check-in calls automatically pause during vacation periods
  • Calls resume automatically on the return date
  • Dashboard shows who's on vacation and when they return

Technical & Security

We have multiple safeguards:

  • 99.9% uptime guarantee with redundant systems
  • Automatic failover to backup servers
  • 24/7 monitoring and immediate alert response
  • Emergency contact procedures for extended outages

We can provide daily reports via email or secure file transfer. For deeper integrations with property management systems, contact us to discuss custom solutions.

Billing & Pricing

There is a one-time setup fee of $500. No cancellation fees, no per-call charges. The monthly fee includes everything: unlimited automated calls, text messaging, dashboard access, and support.

We accept all major credit cards and ACH bank transfers. Billing is monthly in advance with automatic payment processing.
